Privacy Policy

I'm not really sure what's supposed to go in a privacy policy lmao but here it is. This app does not store any user data. Signing in is purely optional. The only benefit to signing in is that you can add your followed artists and the mini song player might be more likely to be able to play the song connecting two artists (this is because of some market region shenanigans). If you do sign in, it only gives access to your followed artists, and your most basic account information like your email, profile pic, subscription tier, etc. I don't take any of that account info, just your followed artists when you click the button.

You can view the source code for the client side app here. There is also a small api that is used to get a Spotify access token that has to be separate so that my client secret isn't exposed. There's not much to it, but you can find the code here.
